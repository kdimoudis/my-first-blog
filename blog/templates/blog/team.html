Meet our Team

Our team of scientists, researchers, film makers, and technical staff have been working together for over twenty years. We have brought many hours of quality video to the screen, and have contributed immeasurably to the scientific community.

Mal, Co-Founder and CEO

Co-Founder and CEO Mal has been leading the Bttrfly team since its inception. He brings his passion for wildlife and decades of filmmaking experience to each project.

Zoe, Co-Founder and CFO

Co-Founder Zoe brings her numerical wizardry to play to ensure that Bttrfly Productions remains financially viable so that we can continue our important mission.

Dr. Simon, Chief Scientist

Dr. Simon is a recognized leader in African wildlife. His expertise ensures that everything we produce is not only scienfitically accurate, but also that our productions leave as minimal an impact on the wildlife we're observing as possible.
