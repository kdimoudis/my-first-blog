Meet Our Team

Our team of musicians, classical music lovers, journalists, and technical staff have been working together for over ten years. We have brought many quality reviews to the public and have contributed to designing and producing numerous live events.

Our project

The Serres Classical Music Magazine project is our latest and most ambitious project to date, but we have worked on similar projects in the past. Our award winning work has been featured in ERT documentaries and in other educational materials. Find out more about our projects...
